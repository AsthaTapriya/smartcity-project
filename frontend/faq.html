❓ FAQ & Help Center
Find answers to common questions.
How do I file a complaint? ▼
Click "File Complaint", fill in your details, describe the problem, upload a photo if possible, and submit. You will receive a unique Complaint ID instantly.
How long does resolution take? ▼
Roads — 7 days. Water Supply — 3 days. Electricity — 2 days. Sanitation — 4 days. Parks — 5 days. If not resolved in time, it is automatically escalated.
What is a Complaint ID? ▼
A unique number assigned to every complaint (e.g. 1, 2, 3...). Use it on the Track Complaint page to see status and full timeline — no login required to track.
What if my complaint is not resolved on time? ▼
If a complaint crosses its SLA deadline, it is automatically escalated to a senior officer and admin is notified. You will see the status change on the tracking page.
Can I file a complaint without registering? ▼
No, you need to register once to file a complaint. However, you can track any complaint using just the Complaint ID without logging in.
What is AI department auto-detection? ▼
When you select "Auto Detect (AI)" in the department field, our AI reads your complaint text and assigns it to the most appropriate department automatically.
